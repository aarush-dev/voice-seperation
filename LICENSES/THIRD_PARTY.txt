Corpora
-------

No corpus data is committed. WSJ0 (LDC93S6A) is LDC-licensed and cannot be redistributed;
obtain it through your own LDC license. LibriSpeech is available from https://openslr.org/12.
Everything WSJ0-derived -- mixtures, references, separated estimates -- is excluded for the
same reason. The committed .npz caches contain metrics only, no audio.
